Bijou For You Case Study

Overview


Bijou for You was the result of a Le Wagon Montréal project. My team was tasked with building an Airbnb-style rental platform using Ruby on Rails, HTML, JavaScript, PostgreSQL, & CSS. Although the website works, the listings aren't currently available for rental.

The Problem

Modern fashionistas enjoy putting on the Ritz for the right occasion but this can get quite expensive. For jewelry, it doesn't make sense to invest a large amount of money in pieces that are rarely worn. Instead, rent from our trusted community of high-end jewelry owners.

Users & Audience

Our user persona is a 30-something female who gets invited to 6 or more weddings a year. Her social calendar is busy but funds are tight. She is tech-savvy and well-connected.

Roles & Responsibilities

I worked on the front-end development for this project.

Scope & Constraints

This project was time-boxed to one week. We needed to have a working MVP in time to present to our coding colleagues.

Outcomes & What I Learned

This project was instrumental in learning front-end development. It forced me to tinker with < div's > until I got everything right. Fighting flex-boxes? You betcha.
